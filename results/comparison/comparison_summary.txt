FEDERATED TRAINING COMPARISON: From Scratch vs Fine-Tuned
============================================================

From Scratch: 700 episodes, ~10h 27min
Fine-Tuned:   200 episodes, ~2h 58min (from cooperative weights)

Metric                                   Scratch   Fine-Tuned
------------------------------------------------------------
Final Avg Queue (last 20 eps)               0.26         0.19
Final Avg Wait Time (last 20)               0.05s         0.04s
Final Avg Reward (last 20)               -3938.5      -3861.5
Episodes to Queue < 1.0                       17           10
Training Time                            10h 27m       2h 58m
Intra-zone FedAvg                             70           20
Inter-zone FedAvg                             28            8

Training Speedup: 3.5x faster with fine-tuning

Conclusion: Fine-tuning from cooperative 4-intersection weights
provides a strong initialization, enabling the 8-intersection
federated system to converge faster with comparable or better
final performance.
